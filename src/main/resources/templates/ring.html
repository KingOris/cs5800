Ring
I designed this ring using 3Dmax. The compositions of this ring are three pyramids overlapping each other. For the largest pyramid which also functions as a base I used the "twist" modifier and added 30 segments on its height. For the pyramids on left and right I used the modifiers "bend", and "FFD 4*4" respectively. I didn't want my ring to look symmetrical, that's why I used different modifiers and different sizes for the pyramids on left and right. After I created the basic formation of the ring. I applied three kinds of materials for the three pyramids. I applied silver to the largest pyramid, gold for the one on the left and polished copper for the one on the right. After applying the materials to the ring, I created an environment and put the ring inside it. I chose to create a basement with no windows and minimum lighting but the ring can still shine within the room since it's made of metals and I put a free light on top of the ring. I made a wooden table so that I can put the ring onto it. Then for better visual effects I made another metallic base for the ring so that I can see a reflection of the ring. I made a twin-size bed just next to the table. The bed also needs some lighting so I put four small light bulbs on the wall next to the bed. I also made a pillow using the rubber material. For the flooring of the room, I chose hard-wood flooring style and I attached black wallpapers for all the walls of the room.
