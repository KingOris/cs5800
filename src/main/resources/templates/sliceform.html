Sliceform
For the Container or Sliceform design. I used six cut wooden pieces with different cuts through them to build. The container I made is not a cube but a strange shaped box. 5 of 6 of its surfaces have uneven heights. Only one side of the box have even height. In order to do that, I had to make the cuts at different positions and with different deepness for all 6 wooden pieces. The cuts are made using a laser-cutting machine at the College of Art. The Laser machine takes an exported file from AutoCAD for execution. I drew the cuts using the "offset" and "trim" commands in AutoCAD. The square I drew in AutoCAD has the exact size with the wooden pieces in real life. That's how I can fit all the cut pieces as planned.
