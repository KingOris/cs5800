Mobius
For the Mobius project, I first learned in class that a Mobius Strip or Mobius band is a surface with only one side. You can draw a closed loop on a Mobius strip without stopping or changing side since it only has one side. Then I used AutoCAD and the knowledge of basic geometry to create a Mobius strip of my own. The images on the right is the Mobius band I made. In AutoCAD, I drew the curves on the left by finding the intersection points of diagonals and middle-dividing lines within the square. And for the semicircle on the right I set its diameter to be half of the length of one of the sides of the square. After printing the plot exported from AutoCAD. I used a scissor to cut along the curves I drew, and finally used tapes to made the Mobius strip.
